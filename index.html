What Is TRIBE?

TRIBE is a community-driven token built on Bitcoin Cash to reward those who build, share, and grow the BCH ecosystem through content and creativity. Whether you're making memes, threads, videos, tools, or tutorials — if you're pushing BCH forward, TRIBE is for you.

Why TRIBE?

  • No middlemen — TRIBE rewards come from the community, for the community.
  • BCH-friendly — TRIBE lives on the Bitcoin Cash chain: fast, cheap, and borderless.
  • Contribution-based — Create value, help BCH grow, and earn TRIBE. Simple.
  • Built for creators — We believe content is the front line of adoption.
  • TRIBE = Recognition — Not for hype, but for impact.

Liquidity Fund

To ensure trading and real use, 2,000 TRIBE (20%) is reserved for early supporters/investors who help provide BCH liquidity on Cauldron DEX. Drop your content in the Telegram, or tag us on X. If it contributes, it earns.

How to Earn TRIBE

TRIBE rewards anyone adding value to the BCH community through content:

  • Memes that hit
  • Threads that teach
  • Videos that explain
  • Tools that help
  • Art that speaks

Every week, 100 TRIBE will be distributed to those helping BCH thrive.
